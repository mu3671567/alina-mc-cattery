About Alina MC Cattery

Welcome to Alina MC Cattery, where we specialize in breeding exceptional Maine Coon cats with champion bloodlines. Our cats are raised with love, care, and the highest standards of health and temperament.

Stress-free Environment

Our Maine Coons live with us in the house with no cages, enjoying freedom and safety in our specially designed home.

Premium Nutrition

Carefully selected and balanced raw meat diet prepared with the highest quality ingredients from trusted sources.

Health First

Regular veterinary check-ups, genetic testing, heart checks, and vaccinations ensure optimal health.

Selective Breeding

Only physically healthy and genetically pure producers. Strict monitoring of all breeding processes.
